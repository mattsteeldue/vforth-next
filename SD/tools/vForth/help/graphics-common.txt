    GRAPHICS-COMMON  --

Shared core for the LAYERxx-GRAPHICS modules:
colour constants, LAYER!, COORD-CHECK, the
vectored primitives (PLOT XPLOT POINT PIXELADD
PIXELATT XY-RATIO EDGE), the LAYER: defining
word, and the layer-independent DRAW-LINE
CIRCLE PAINT .INK .PAPER ...

Loaded automatically by NEEDS LAYERxx-GRAPHICS;
rarely needs NEEDS GRAPHICS-COMMON on its own.
