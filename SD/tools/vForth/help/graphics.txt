    GRAPHICS  --

NEEDS GRAPHICS includes a few definitions.
  LAYER0  LAYER10  LAYER11  LAYER12  LAYER13  LAYER2
  ATTRIB  CIRCLE   PLOT     PAINT    DRAW-LINE
  .INK    .PAPER   .BRIGHT  .FLASH
  .OVER   .BORDER  .INVERSE

To load just one mode (smaller dictionary use)
NEEDS LAYERxx-GRAPHICS instead, e.g.
  NEEDS LAYER2-GRAPHICS
See HELP GRAPHICS-COMMON.
