    LAYER22-GRAPHICS  --

NEEDS LAYER22-GRAPHICS loads GRAPHICS-COMMON and
activates Layer 2 high-res: 320x256 pixels, 256
colours, 1 colour per pixel.  Uses 80K of RAM (ten
8K MMU7 pages) laid out in vertical bands.
Same PLOT/DRAW-LINE/CIRCLE as the other modes;
x = vertical 0..255, y = horizontal 0..319.
